About Veg foods

The practice of not eating meat is known as vegetarianism (red meat, poultry, seafood, insects, and the flesh of any other animal). It could also mean avoiding eating any leftovers from animal slaughter.

One explanation is that many vegetarians have a tendency to eat a lot of fresh, wholesome, plant-based meals, which are rich in antioxidants and fiber. People who choose to eat vegetarianism frequently become more engaged in making general healthy decisions.
