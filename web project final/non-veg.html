About Non-Veg foods

Food that is not vegetarian contains meat and occasionally eggs. Non-vegetarians are those who eat meat, as opposed to vegetarians. The majority of people consume a non-vegetarian diet worldwide. In nutritional science, non-vegetarians are sometimes referred to as omnivores.
